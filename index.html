ABOUT ME

My name is Calvin Kling and I'm a Web Developer.


I grew up in the Midwest swimming in lakes, gaming on the Nintendo 64 and coloring within the lines ahead of my age group. My interests varied throughout the years and it was difficult to decide on which career path I wanted to take as I got older, so I found myself undecided throughout college at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. After graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Communication Arts and a Certificate of Entrepreneurship, I worked in staffing as a Recruiter and in customer service for life insurance, yet felt unfulfilled.


My interest in the tech field stems from before I knew what it even was. As a child, I would spend hours playing Nintendo games, creating my own characters or side stories I wished were in the games, wondering how it all actually came together. As I grew up and watch technology evolve around me, the fascination of what is possible to happen next hasn't changed. I'm excited to finally jump into this new chapter with both feet!
